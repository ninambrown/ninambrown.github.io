I'm a second-year Astrophysics Ph.D. student at the University of Arizona working with Chad Bender. I'm largely interested in spectrograph design and usage for exoplanet detections and characterization. 

I'm currently working on reduce/reuse/recycling the retired spectrograph CSHELL, to use as the main nIR spectrograph for the Large Fiber Array Spectroscopic Telescope (LFAST).
